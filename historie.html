About Us

We, the Berlin Recycling Volleys, have forged a storied history since our establishment in 1991. Over more than three decades, we have grown from a promising club into one of Germany's most accomplished volleyball teams. Our journey is marked by numerous triumphs in both domestic and international arenas, cementing our reputation as a powerhouse in European volleyball. From our early years, we have consistently demonstrated our commitment to excellence, capturing multiple German Bundesliga titles and establishing ourselves as a dominant force within the league. Our relentless pursuit of success led us to compete regularly in prestigious European competitions such as the CEV Champions League, where we have showcased our skill and determination on an international stage. Throughout our history, we have not only celebrated victories but also faced challenges that have shaped us into the resilient team we are today. Each season has brought new opportunities to strengthen our bond as a team and to inspire our loyal fans with thrilling performances. As we continue to build on our legacy, we remain dedicated to upholding the values of sportsmanship, teamwork, and dedication that define the Berlin Recycling Volleys. Our history is a testament to our passion for the sport and our unwavering commitment to achieving greatness, both on and off the volleyball court.
